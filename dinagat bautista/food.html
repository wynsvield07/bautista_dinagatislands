Local Food & Delicacies

Kinhasong Law-oy

Kinhasong Law-oy (specifically Kinhasong Law-oy ala Dinagat) is a nutritious, budget-friendly, and award-winning Visayan vegetable soup, specifically from the Dinagat Islands in the Philippines. It is a variation of the traditional Law-oy or Utan Bisaya (vegetable soup) that features kinhason (shellfish/mussels) instead of the usual dried fish or shrimp, making it a sustainable and locally sourced dish.

Adobong Sahang

Adobong sahang (or saang) is a Filipino dish featuring spider conch meat simmered in a mixture of soy sauce, vinegar, garlic, onions, and peppercorns. As a popular Visayan delicacy, the chewy, protein-rich seashell meat is commonly prepared this way

Linat-ang Isda

Linat-ang Isda is fish in ginger Broth is a type of Filipino clear soup dish wherein fish such as Tuna is cooked in a broth composed of ginger, lemongrass and other vegetables
