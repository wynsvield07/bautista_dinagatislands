Welcome to Dinagat Islands

Dinagat Islands is a beautiful province located in the Caraga Region of the Philippines. The island is known for its clear blue waters, white sand beaches, limestone formations, and peaceful natural environment. It is one of the country’s hidden gems that offers unforgettable experiences for tourists who love nature and adventure.

Visitors can enjoy island hopping, snorkeling, hiking scenic viewpoints, and tasting delicious local seafood. Dinagat Islands is the perfect destination for travelers who want to relax, explore nature, and experience the beauty of the Philippines.
