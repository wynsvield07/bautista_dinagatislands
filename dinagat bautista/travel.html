Travel Guide

How To Get There

Travelers can fly to Surigao City and take a ferry going to Dinagat Islands.

Best Time To Visit

The best time to visit is during the dry season from March to June.

Accommodation

Visitors can stay in local resorts, guest houses, and beach cottages.
