Local Food & Delicacies

Taste the unique flavors of Dinagat Islands

Kinhasong Law-oy

Kinhasong Law-oy is a nutritious Visayan vegetable soup from Dinagat Islands made with shellfish instead of dried fish, creating a sustainable and locally sourced dish.

Adobong Sahang

Adobong sahang is a Visayan dish made from spider conch cooked in soy sauce, vinegar, garlic, and spices.

Linat-ang Isda

A clear fish soup cooked with ginger, lemongrass, and vegetables, giving it a refreshing and aromatic flavor.
