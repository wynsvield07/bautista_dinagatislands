Travel Guide

Everything you need to know before visiting Dinagat Islands

How To Get There

Travelers usually fly to Surigao City. From there, you can ride a ferry or passenger boat going to Dinagat Islands. The boat ride usually takes about 45 minutes to 1 hour depending on weather conditions.

Best Time To Visit

The best time to visit Dinagat Islands is during the dry season from March to June. During these months, the weather is sunny and perfect for beach activities, island hopping, and exploring the natural attractions.

Accommodation

Visitors can stay in local resorts, guest houses, and beach cottages across the island. These accommodations offer peaceful environments and beautiful ocean views at affordable prices.

Travel Tips

Bring Cash

Many small stores and resorts on the island may not accept credit cards.

Respect Nature

Help preserve the island's beauty by not leaving trash and respecting local wildlife.

Plan Island Tours

Island hopping tours are one of the best ways to explore Dinagat Islands.
