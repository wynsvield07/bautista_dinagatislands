Tourist Attractions

Discover the natural beauty of Dinagat Islands

Mount Redondo Natural Bonsai Forest

The Mount Redondo Natural Bonsai Forest in Loreto features naturally stunted trees creating a rare ecological landscape.

Blue Lagoon

A hidden natural pool in Libjo surrounded by limestone cliffs and turquoise waters.

Campintac Black Beach No. 2

A historic beach where the U.S. Army Rangers landed during WWII before the Battle of Leyte.
